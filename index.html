Why nHct?

The Problem: Plasma Refill

Traditional Hematocrit is unreliable during diuresis because plasma refills from the interstitial space between doses.

Plasma fluctuates, but RBC mass is constant.

The Solution: Normalized Hct

nHct calculates the endpoint based on the patient's fixed Red Blood Cell mass. By targeting nHct, you ensure the patient reaches true euvolemia regardless of plasma shifting.

Key Insight:

Removing plasma reduces Total Volume, which naturally raises Hematocrit percentages.
